what is grimace?

Grimace is a fluent way to generate regular expression strings. It was inspired by the following:

  • I use regular expressions often enough to know most of the syntax, but infrequently enough to never be able to remember the details
  • An article in Dr Dobbs journal about using the C/C++ preprocessor to make it easier to write regular expressions
  • A vague interest in fluent syntax and how one might implement such things in a Python/functional way
